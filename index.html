West Side United: Community Health

Average life expectancy in Chicago's loop is 85 years, however, if you take the train a few stops into the west side, life expectancy drops to 69 years.

This 16-year life expectancy gap exists due to inequities in education, physical environment, economic vitality, and healthcare.

In order to address the social determinants of health, West Side United will focus on foundational elements and existing conditions for hospitals to strengthen their impact through a collective set of community health initiatives.
